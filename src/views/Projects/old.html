Food Court, a Community-based Delivery App
TritonByte Team
|
May 2019 - Present

Food Court is a community-based food delivery app that aims to provide well-prepared and affordable food for college students. We have connected with local restaurants for the service similar to food catering, but have fixed delivery locations, more diversity food options, and increased order quantities.

Besides the mobile app, we are also developing a mobile app for drivers and a web platform for restaurants for better management.

The front ends are implemented using React-Native and React, while the backend is using Node, Apollo, and GraphQL.
